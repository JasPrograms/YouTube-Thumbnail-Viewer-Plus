Privacy Policy for YouTube-Thumbnail-Viewer-Plus

Introduction

YouTube-Thumbnail-Viewer-Plus ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension that displays the YouTube thumbnail of the video you are currently watching. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

Website Content: The Extension collects the URL and thumbnail image of the YouTube video you are currently watching to display the thumbnail on the sidebar.

How We Use Your Information

The collected URL and thumbnail image are used solely to provide the main functionality of the Extension, which is to display the YouTube thumbnail in the sidebar and offer a download link.

Data Storage and Security

The Extension does not store any data on remote servers. All data is processed locally within your browser. We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to any third parties.

Third-Party Services

The Extension may use third-party services, such as content delivery networks (CDNs) for hosting the extension files. These third parties do not have access to your data.

User Control

You can disable or uninstall the Extension at any time via the Chrome extensions settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
